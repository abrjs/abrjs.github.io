Andrea Barajas is a criminology researcher focused on restorative justice diversion and community accountability in California.

She currently evaluates youth diversion pilots with county probation partners, measuring how survivor care, program fidelity, and community governance affect long-run outcomes. Her mixed-methods practice braids administrative data with narrative interviews to build accountability frameworks rooted in healing, not punitive metrics.
